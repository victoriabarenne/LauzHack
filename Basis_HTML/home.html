     In the midst of the COVID19 pandemic, the world we know has changed and a lot of new measures have been introduced. To make it easier to understand the current situation and not get overwhelmed, our goal is to model the pandemic spread. Due to the currently incomplete sets of data on COVID19, we will be studying more general epidemics using two different models: SIS and SIRS.
