About

We want to highlight the fact that we are not epidemiologist, those are toy models with a pedagological objective. We should guard you to make any precise asumption on a specific case from this website datas. We made simplification in our models and those cannot be used to predict actual data. In the other hand we do want to transmit key ideas of epidemic propagation. If a specialist see a misunderstanding in one of our explanation we are glad to discuss it with him.

This site and models were developed in a bit less than three days by four EPFL (Swiss Federal Instite of Technology Lausanne) student in the context of the "LauzHack against Covid-19" hackathon during the 2020 Covid-19 crisis :
