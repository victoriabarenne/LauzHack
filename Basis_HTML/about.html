About

This website and the models were developed in 3 days by 4 EPFL Bachelor students studying Mathematics during the "LauzHack against Covid-19" hackathon in the midst of the 2020 COVID-19 crisis.

While we are not epidemiologists, we have thus provided a generalised model of a pandemic. This model is intended for pedagogical purposes, and the simplifications involved in the modeling process prevent our model from being an exact predictor of the actual trend. Nevertheless, we hope to have achieved our goal of conveying the key ideas of epidemic propagation. We would be glad to discuss errata in our interpretation of the epidemiological modeling with specialists.

For detailed explanations about the mathematical models we used and their implementation, check out our report:
Notes on PandemicAnalytics
